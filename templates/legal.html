Privacy Policy
Status: 10.06.2025
We appreciate your interest in our web application go2klo.com. The protection of your personal data is very important to us. Below, we inform you about the collection, processing, and use of your data within the scope of using our web app and website.
storage
Collection and Storage of Personal Data, and Type and Purpose of Their Use
  • When visiting the website
    When you access our website go2klo.com, the browser used on your device automatically sends information to our website's server. This information is temporarily stored in a so-called log file. The following information is collected without your intervention and stored until automated deletion:
    • IP address of the requesting computer,
    • Date and time of access,
    • Name and URL of the retrieved file,
    • Website from which access is made (referrer URL),
    • Browser used and, if applicable, the operating system of your computer, as well as the name of your access provider.
    The mentioned data are processed exclusively for the following purposes:
    • Ensuring a smooth connection setup of the website,
    • Ensuring comfortable and secure use of our website,
    • Evaluation of system security and stability, and
    • For purely administrative purposes.
    The legal basis for data processing is Art. 6 para. 1 S. 1 lit. f GDPR. Our legitimate interest arises from the data collection purposes listed above. There is no use of the collected data for analysis purposes (tracking) or for other purposes that do not serve the operation of the web app. Under no circumstances do we use the collected data to draw conclusions about your person.
  • Upon registration and use of the web app (account creation)
    If you create a user account on go2klo.com, we collect and store only the data necessary for using the app:
    • Username and email adress
    • Password (stored exclusively in encrypted form/as a hash)
    • Date and time of registration
    These registration data are used exclusively for logging in and for providing the functions of your user account. The legal basis for this is Art. 6 para. 1 S. 1 lit. b GDPR (fulfillment of contract or execution of pre-contractual measures).
  • When submitting ratings, adding toilets, and uploading photos
    When you submit ratings, add new toilets, or upload photos, we store the information you provide exclusively for internal site purposes:
    • Content of the rating (comment, rating)
    • Details of added toilets (location, etc.)
    • Link to your user account (so your contributions can be assigned and the app's functions are ensured)
    • Date and time of the contribution
    This data serves exclusively the core functionality of our web app, namely the provision and display of user-generated toilet information for other users and to ensure community functions. Your ratings and added toilets may be visible to other users (pseudonymized via your username, unless you use real names). There is no further processing or use of this data outside of the app's functionality. The legal basis is Art. 6 para. 1 S. 1 lit. b GDPR (fulfillment of contract).
  • Upon contact (e.g., by email)
    If you contact us by email, the data transmitted by you (e.g., your email address, your name, and the content of your message) will be stored for the purpose of processing your request. The legal basis for this is Art. 6 para. 1 S. 1 lit. b GDPR.

Cookies
We use exclusively technically necessary cookies on our website, without which the functionality of our website cannot be guaranteed. These are small files that your browser automatically creates and that are stored on your device (laptop, tablet, smartphone, etc.) when you visit our site. Cookies do not cause any damage to your device, do not contain viruses, Trojans, or other malware.
These cookies serve to enable the core functions of the website (e.g., to save login status or to ensure connection security). Information generated by these cookies is not intended to analyze your browsing behavior, display personalized advertising, or create profiles about you.
The data processed by technically necessary cookies are necessary for the aforementioned purposes to protect our legitimate interests in accordance with Art. 6 para. 1 S. 1 lit. f GDPR.
Deactivating these cookies by the user is not possible, as this would impair the basic functionality of the web app.
